ABOUT US

Pet Haven was founded with a simple mission: to create a welcoming and compassionate environment for pets and pet owners alike. Established in the early 2000s by animal lovers who wanted a better alternative to traditional pet shops, Pet Haven aimed to combine pet adoption, quality pet products, and services like grooming and training under one roof. Originally, Pet Haven started as a small community-focused store that worked with local animal shelters to promote pet adoption and responsible pet ownership. As word spread, it became known not just as a store but as a community resourceâ€”a place where people could find not only supplies but also advice, support, and education on caring for their pets. Today, Pet Haven has expanded to several locations, offering a wide range of products and services, including pet grooming, training, veterinary consultations, and even pet daycare. The brand remains true to its roots, partnering with shelters and hosting adoption events to help pets find loving homes. With its commitment to quality and care, Pet Haven has become a trusted name in pet services and continues to grow while keeping the health and happiness of pets at the forefront of everything it does.
